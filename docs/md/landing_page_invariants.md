## 2. Problem

### Punch
Puffiness is not random — it’s fluid stagnation.

### Text
The face does not have its own lymphatic pump. Fluid moves only when tissues move. If the neck and jaw stay tense, lymph drainage slows down and fluid accumulates overnight. That’s why many women wake up with swelling under the eyes, heaviness in the lower face, or a blurred jawline.

### Required Visual
Simple anatomical animation or diagram showing lymph flow from face to neck.

The protocol starts with gentle relaxation of the jaw and neck muscles — the main drainage pathways. Only after tension is released do we guide fluid through the natural lymphatic routes of the face.
